For the Chicken Marinade:
500 grams boneless chicken thighs or breasts, thinly sliced

3 tbsp plain yogurt

3 tbsp olive oil

4 garlic cloves, minced

2 tsp ground cumin

2 tsp ground paprika

1 tsp ground turmeric

1 tsp ground coriander

1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)

Juice of 1 lemon

Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:
Pita bread or flatbreads

Sliced onions, tomatoes, cucumbers

Fresh parsley

Pickles (optional)

Garlic sauce or tahini sauce

Instructions:
Marinate chicken:
In a bowl, combine yogurt, olive oil, garlic, spices, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
Add the sliced chicken and mix well. Marinate for at least 1-2 hours (overnight is best).

Cook chicken:
Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
Cook the marinated chicken slices until fully cooked and slightly charred (about 5-7 minutes per side).

Assemble shawarma:
Warm the pita bread.
Place cooked chicken on the bread, add sliced onions, tomatoes, cucumbers, parsley, and pickles.
Drizzle with garlic sauce or tahini sauce.

Wrap and serve:
Roll up the pita to enclose the fillings. Serve immediately.

Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin (kaddu), peeled and grated or finely chopped

1 liter full-fat milk

3/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)

1/4 cup rice (washed and soaked for 30 minutes)

4-5 green cardamoms, crushed

10-12 cashew nuts

10-12 almonds, sliced

1 tbsp ghee

A pinch of saffron strands (optional)

1 tbsp raisins (optional)

Instructions:
Roast nuts and raisins:
Heat ghee in a pan. Lightly roast cashew nuts, almonds, and raisins until golden. Remove and set aside.

Cook pumpkin:
In the same pan, add grated pumpkin and sauté for 5-7 minutes until soft and fragrant.

Cook rice:
Drain soaked rice. Add it to the pan with pumpkin and sauté for 2 minutes.

Boil milk:
In a heavy-bottomed pot, bring milk to a boil.
Add the pumpkin and rice mixture to the boiling milk.

Simmer:
Cook on low heat, stirring frequently to avoid sticking, until the rice and pumpkin are cooked and the milk thickens (about 30-40 minutes).

Add sugar and cardamom:
Add sugar and crushed cardamoms. Stir well and cook for another 5-7 minutes.

Add nuts and saffron:
Add roasted nuts, raisins, and saffron strands (if using). Mix well.

Serve:
Serve warm or chilled, garnished with extra nuts if desired.

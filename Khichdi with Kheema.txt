Ingredients:
For Kheema (Minced Meat Curry):
300 grams minced meat (chicken, mutton, or beef)

1 large onion, finely chopped

2 tomatoes, chopped

2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)

1 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp red chili powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1/2 tsp garam masala powder

Salt to taste

2-3 tbsp oil

Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Khichdi:
1 cup rice

1/2 cup moong dal (yellow split gram), washed

4 cups water (adjust as needed)

1 tsp cumin seeds

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Salt to taste

1-2 tbsp ghee or oil

Instructions:
Prepare Kheema:
Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté till raw smell disappears.

Add tomatoes and cook until soft.

Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.

Add minced meat, stir, and cook on medium heat until meat is browned and cooked through (about 15-20 minutes).

Sprinkle garam masala and mix. Garnish with fresh coriander. Set aside.

Prepare Khichdi:
Rinse rice and moong dal well.

In a pressure cooker or pot, heat ghee/oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

Add turmeric powder and washed rice and dal. Stir for a minute.

Add water and salt. Cook until soft and mushy (pressure cook for 3-4 whistles or cook on stove until done).

Ingredients:
For the Kunafa:
250 grams kataifi (shredded phyllo dough) or shredded phyllo pastry

200 grams sweet cheese (like mozzarella, ricotta, or akkawi cheese soaked and desalinated)

100 grams unsalted butter, melted

1/4 cup sugar (optional, to sprinkle)

For the Sugar Syrup (Attar):
2 cups sugar

1 1/2 cups water

1 tbsp lemon juice

1 tbsp rose water or orange blossom water (optional)

Garnish:
Crushed pistachios

Ground cardamom (optional)

Instructions:
Prepare sugar syrup:
In a saucepan, combine sugar and water.
Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10-15 minutes until slightly thickened.
Add lemon juice and rose/orange blossom water.
Remove from heat and let it cool.

Prepare kunafa dough:
If using kataifi, gently shred and loosen the strands with your hands.
Pour melted butter over the shredded dough and mix well to coat evenly.

Assemble:
Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
Grease a round baking dish or kunafa tray.
Spread half of the buttered shredded dough evenly in the dish and press gently.
Spread the cheese evenly over this layer.
Cover with the remaining shredded dough and press gently again.

Bake:
Bake for 30-40 minutes until the top is golden brown and crisp.

Add syrup:
Remove from oven and immediately pour the cooled sugar syrup evenly over the hot kunafa.

Garnish and serve:
Sprinkle crushed pistachios and a pinch of cardamom on top.
Serve warm.

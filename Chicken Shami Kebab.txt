Ingredients:
500 grams minced chicken (keema)

1/2 cup chana dal (split Bengal gram)

1 large onion, finely chopped

2-3 green chilies, finely chopped

1-inch piece ginger, grated

4-5 garlic cloves, minced

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp red chili powder

1 tsp garam masala powder

1 tsp cumin powder

1/2 tsp coriander powder

1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped

1 egg (optional, for binding)

Salt to taste

Oil or ghee for shallow frying

Instructions:
Cook chana dal:
Wash and soak chana dal for 1 hour.
Boil dal in water until soft but not mushy. Drain and keep aside.

Cook chicken:
In a pan, cook minced chicken with turmeric, salt, and little water until fully cooked and dry. Let it cool.

Grind mixture:
In a food processor or grinder, add cooked chicken, boiled chana dal, onion, green chilies, ginger, garlic, coriander leaves, and spices (red chili, garam masala, cumin, coriander powder).
Grind into a coarse, slightly sticky mixture. If itâ€™s too dry, add a little water or egg for binding.

Shape kebabs:
Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into round or oval patties.

Fry kebabs:
Heat oil or ghee in a pan.
Shallow fry the patties on medium heat until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side.

Serve:
Serve hot with green chutney, ketchup, or yogurt dip.

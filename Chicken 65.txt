Ingredients:
500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces

2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)

1 tbsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp garam masala

1 tbsp lemon juice

2 tbsp cornflour

2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)

1 egg (optional, helps in binding)

Salt to taste

Curry leaves (a handful)

Oil for deep frying

Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:
Marinate chicken:
In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, salt, cornflour, all-purpose flour, and egg (if using).
Mix well and let it marinate for at least 1 hour.

Heat oil:
Heat enough oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat.

Fry chicken:
Deep fry marinated chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and crispy (about 5-7 minutes).
Remove and drain on paper towels.

Temper with curry leaves (optional):
In the same oil, fry fresh curry leaves until crisp. Drain and add to fried chicken for extra flavor.

Serve:
Garnish with fried curry leaves and fresh coriander. Serve hot as a starter or snack with lemon wedges and chutney.
